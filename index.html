Replacing Jane…

Eva’s world is turned upside down when she learns of her best friend—Jane’s death. Unable to grieve until the killer is brought to justice, Eva sets out to track down infamous vigilante Blank. This short graphic novel is the first volume of the series, and is a debut for author Vincenzo. The book is planned to release in late 2023. Thank you for your interest in the Replacing Jane; stay tuned for future plans for the series and more updates on the release.
